Work and heat

Joule's experiment

Temparature

Quantification of how hot or cold an object is

Specific heat

Amount of heat required for the temparature of 1kg of material to rise by 1 absolute degrees. Q = c dT m, so c = Q / (dT * m)

Heat capacity

Amount of heat required for the temperature of any material of arbitrary mass to rise by 1 absolute degrees. C = c m, so C = Q / dT
