Org-roam

Org-roam is "Plain-text personal knowledge management system". Inspired by Roam, it builds upon Org-mode.

List of shortcuts: org-roam-buffer-toggle: turns buffer on or off. "C-c n l" org-roam-node-find: finds a node, or create one. "C-c n f" org-roam-node-insert: inserts a node, or create one. "C-c n i"
