Ring(Mathematics)

A ring is a set S with two binary operators + and * (addition and multiplication) satisfying the following conditions:

  1. Additive associativity
  2. Additive commutativity
  3. Additive identity
  4. Additive inverse
  5. Left and right distributivity
  6. Multiplicative associativity
