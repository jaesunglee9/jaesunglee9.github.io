Analog & digital

First we have Natural Signal, that is Continuous-time and Continuous-range This signal Then processed Using 1. Discretization(Sampling) and then 2. Quantization. Then the signal becomes Discrete-time and Discrete-range. The former is commonly called analog, whereas the latter is called digital.

Integer: Repeated division Decimal: Repeated multiplication
